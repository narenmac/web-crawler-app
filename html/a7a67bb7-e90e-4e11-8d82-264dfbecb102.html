      The countdown to Coachella weekend 1 begins!

      By The YouTube Team

      Apr 10, 2025 – [[read-time]] minute read

      Live creators, merch, and more. Here's what you can expect all weekend long.

      Coachella 2025 is almost here! The livestream schedule is now available and kicks off Friday, April 11 at 4 p.m. PT / 7 p.m. ET on Coachella’s YouTube channel. All weekend long fans can tune into stages including, Main Stage, Outdoor Theater, Sahara, Mojave, Gobi, and Sonora. Once the headlining sets are complete, that day’s performances will loop on repeat until the next day’s sets begin. Set your reminders and join the stream on Coachella’s YouTube channel.

      Fans watch Coachella on YouTube from all over the world. In fact, more than half of the views on the official Coachella channel came from outside the U.S. for three years running.* We know you want access to the festival fun, and we’ve got you covered. We’re introducing new, exclusive content you won't want to miss, wherever you are. Here’s what you can expect:

      Watch With, exclusively on YouTube

      For the first time ever, you can watch your favorite artists’ performance alongside your favorite creator. These livestreams will be available on the creators’ channels on mobile, desktop and TV.

      Weekend 1

      Lady Gaga x Zach Campbell: April 11 at 11:10 p.m. PT

      Alok x Blogueirinha: April 12 at 4:10 p.m. PT

      Rawayana x SKabeche: April 12 at 11:00 p.m. PT

      Weekend 2

      Lady Gaga x NAOMI CLUB: April 18 at 11:10 p.m. PT

      ENHYPEN x Ha Sisters: April 19 at 8:35 p.m. PT

      Merch drops

      YouTube Shopping is making it easier than ever to bring the festival merch booth to you. Snag official merchandise from Coachella and over a dozen artists, including Lady Gaga, Charli xcx, Circle Jerks, Clairo, ENHYPEN, GloRilla, JENNIE, Junior H, LISA, Megan Thee Stallion, Missy Elliott, T-Pain, The Marías, and Tyla without leaving the livestream. Whether you're on mobile, desktop, or watching on your TV, all you have to do is to click the shopping button on screen or scan the QR code.

      Lifestyle livestream

      Top creators will give you a behind-the-scenes look at Coachella, including artist interviews, commentary, games, and more. Tune in live on the creator’s channels. During weekend 1, you'll see live streams from:

      Friday: Terrell starting at 5 p.m. PT

      Saturday: Jake Shane + special guest Alix Earle starting at 5:30 p.m. PT

      Sunday: Alisha Marie, Remi Cruz, Zane Hijazi, Heath Hussar starting at 4:30 p.m. PT

      Nothing brings the world together like music, so get ready to embrace the energy, discover new artists, and vibe out with music fans from every corner of the globe, wherever you are, only on YouTube.

      *Source: YouTube data
