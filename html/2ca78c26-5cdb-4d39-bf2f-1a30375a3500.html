About us

VMware Tanzu Labs works with organizations worldwide to accelerate the delivery of software and modernize existing apps, while reducing operating costs and risk. All of our projects use remote collaboration, from accommodating a single participant in another location, to full remote delivery. We’ve established practices for agile software delivery in a remote context based on years of experience. We don’t just drop in, deploy, and leave— we work side by side with your team (even virtually) to build capabilities and instill a process that shows immediate and lasting impact. We help empower your teams to deliver the products your customers need, regardless of their location.
